About Zarsa Gold

For over a decade, Zarsa Gold has crafted bespoke fine jewelry rooted in timeless design and meticulous workmanship. We work closely with each client to design, prototype, and hand-finish pieces that carry meaning — from engagement rings to heirloom bracelets and statement necklaces. Every piece is made in-house with premium 18K/22K gold.

  • Bespoke design consultations
  • CAD prototyping and custom sizing
  • Lifetime care & complimentary cleaning
